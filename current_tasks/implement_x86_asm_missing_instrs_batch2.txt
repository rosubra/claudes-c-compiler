Implement batch 2 of missing x86 builtin assembler instructions.

Add encodings for:
- clc, stc, cld (flag manipulation)
- cpuid, syscall, sahf, lahf
- push/pop without q suffix
- bsrl, bsfl, bsrq, bsfq (bit scan)
- bswap without suffix
- mull, mulw (unsigned multiply)
- rcrl, rcll, rcrq, rclq (rotate through carry)
- btl, btsl, btrl, btcl, btq, btsq, btrq, btcq (bit test/set/reset/complement)
- shldl, shrdl (32-bit double shifts), plus %cl forms
- notb, notw, negb, negw, incw, incb, decw, decb (unary 8/16-bit)
- mov/add/sub/xor/etc without suffix (size inference from operands)
- jnc, jrcxz, loop (branch instructions)
- pushf, pushfq, fninit
- movsq, rep movsq, rep stosq, stosq
- lock cmpxchg16b
- MMX register support (mm0-mm7)
- Segment register support (cs, ds, es, fs, gs, ss)
- AVX: vmovdqa, vmovdqu, vbroadcastss, vpand, vpcmpeqd, etc.
- palignr, pshufb, shufps, shufpd, movapd
- xchg without suffix
