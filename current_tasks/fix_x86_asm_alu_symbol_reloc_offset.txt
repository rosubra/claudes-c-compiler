Fix x86 assembler: ALU instructions with symbol immediates (e.g., cmpq $symbol, mem)
double-count the section base offset in relocation offsets.

In encoder.rs lines 2548 and 2567, the relocation offset is computed as:
  self.bytes.len() + self.offset
This includes the section base (self.offset), but elf_writer_common.rs also adds
base_offset, resulting in the relocation pointing beyond the section boundary.

Fix: use self.bytes.len() instead of self.bytes.len() + self.offset.

This breaks the x86 standalone kernel build with:
  ld: vmlinux.o(.entry.text+0x2757): reloc against '.entry.text': error 4
