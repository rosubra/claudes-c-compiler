Fix typeof(sizeof(...)), typeof(alignof(...)), and typeof(enum_constant) resolution

The IR lowerer's get_expr_ctype_lowerer() does not handle Sizeof, Alignof, or enum
constant Identifiers, causing typeof() expressions wrapping these to fail with:
  "could not resolve type of 'typeof' expression; defaulting to 'int'"

This causes ~27 kernel files to fail compilation during defconfig build because
the kernel uses -Werror and these warnings become errors.

Fix: Add Sizeof/Alignof/GnuAlignof/AlignofExpr/GnuAlignofExpr => CType::ULong
(or UInt on 32-bit) and enum constant Identifiers => CType::Int to the
get_expr_ctype_lowerer match arms in src/ir/lowering/expr_types.rs.
